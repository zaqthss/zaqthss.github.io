Biography

Aoqian Zhang is currently a PhD candidate in institute of Information System and Engineering, School of Software, Tsinghua University, under supervison of Prof. Jianmin Wang and Prof. Shaoxu Song. He received his B.E. in 2013 in Computer Software from School of Software, Tsinghua University.

Research Interests

Data Cleaning, Data Imputation and Temporal Anomaly Detection.
